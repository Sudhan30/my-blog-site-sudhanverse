Things That Broke (and How I Fixed Them)

  • Dynamic DNS got flagged by VPNs. Solution: move records and TLS to Cloudflare; reputation + caches improved reachability.
  • Bot noise & crawlers. Solution: basic WAF rules, rate limiting, and a stricter robots policy for non‚Äëcontent paths.
  • Complex routing brain‚Äëtwisters. Solution: drew the traffic flow (client ‚Üí CDN ‚Üí ingress ‚Üí service) and validated each hop with logs.

Lessons You Can Steal
